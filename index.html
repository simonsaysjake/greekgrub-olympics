When the world comes to Los Angeles in the summer of 2028, USC's iconic Row will be at the center of it all. Greek Grub is the private chef and food service company that has called The Row home since 2011 — serving fresh, honest, chef-prepared meals across 11 fraternity and sorority houses. Now, as these houses welcome corporate hosts, international delegations, and VIP guests for the Olympics, we're ready to do what we've always done: keep your house fed, fueled, and taken care of.

The Olympic Reality

Why On-Site Matters During the Games

Los Angeles will welcome over 15 million visitors during the 2028 Games. USC's campus is the official Olympic Media Village, and the Coliseum — just steps from The Row — will host the opening ceremony and track & field events.

The surrounding neighborhood will see unprecedented traffic, demand, and congestion. Restaurants will have weeks-long waitlists. Delivery apps will be unreliable. Having a dedicated, on-site private chef isn't a luxury during the Olympics — it's a necessity.

Meet the Chef

Brian Johnston

Founder & Executive Chef

Greek Grub is a family-owned operation founded by Brian Johnston, a classically trained Executive Chef with a culinary degree and extensive background in fine dining hotels and restaurants. Brian started Greek Grub in 2011 after noticing a lack of professionally trained chefs in the Greek system. Today, Greek Grub brings restaurant-quality meals and full kitchen management to chapter houses across California.
